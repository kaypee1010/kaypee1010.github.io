## About Me

I am an Assistant Professor specializing in **Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science** with academic and industry experience across India and Australia. My work focuses on bridging theoretical research with real-world applications in intelligent systems, cyber-physical environments, and data-driven decision support.

I hold a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence from the **University of Melbourne, Australia**, and a Master’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering (Information Security) from **PEC University of Technology, Chandigarh**.

My professional journey integrates academic teaching, applied research, and industry data engineering experience, enabling me to design curricula and research initiatives aligned with emerging technological demands.

## Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach emphasizes experiential learning, industry relevance, and problem-driven education. I incorporate flipped classroom strategies, case-based learning, and real-world datasets to help students develop analytical thinking and practical implementation skills aligned with Outcome-Based Education (OBE) frameworks.
